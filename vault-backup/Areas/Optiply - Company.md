# Optiply — Company Profile

## What Optiply Does
Inventory optimization SaaS for e-commerce. Automates purchasing/replenishment decisions.

**Core value prop:** "Order the right products, at the right time, in the right quantity."

## Key Metrics (from website)
- 500+ e-commerce companies
- 32% less inventory (avg customer result)
- 80% less "no" to customers (stockout reduction)
- 50% less backorders
- 95% availability of A-products
- 80% faster replenishment process
- 40+ forecasting calculations in the algorithm

## How It Works
1. **Connect data** — e-commerce platform, ERP, WMS, suppliers, pricing software, marketing, weather
2. **AI forecasts** — ML-based demand forecasting using real-time data, seasonality, trends, lead times
3. **Auto-replenishment** — calculates what/when/how much to purchase
4. **Continuous optimization** — learns from actual results, improves forecasts over time

## Target Customers
- E-commerce companies
- Wholesalers
- Retailers
- Typical buyer persona: Purchaser, Operations Manager, CEO of mid-size e-commerce

## Differentiators vs Traditional Inventory Software
- Real-time data + ML vs static min/max + historical only
- Continuous optimization vs one-time setup
- Proactive purchasing vs reactive
- Multi-source data (marketing, weather, etc.) vs sales data only
